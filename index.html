It is Okay to be Here.
Join us on the Oubaitori Collective Blossom Trail as we gather anonymous evidence for the design of a new support service for neurodivergent adults across the North East of England.

Not able to join us? We would still like to hear your views if you are a neurodivergent adult. Your views matter to us!

Stage 1: Reset▼
Begin with a wellness system check-in. Focus on breath, body, and stillness.
1. In the past 12 months, how often have you experienced overwhelm or burnout symptoms that affected your life?
Stage 2: Expansion▼
Move toward expansive views. Reflect on your capacity to grow and create space.
2. What barriers have you faced in accessing appropriate support before or after your neurodivergence assessment?
Stage 3: Flow▼
Allow movement and reflection. 'Be' in the current of your journey.
3. If you become stressed or overwhelmed, how easy is it for you to recover?
Stage 4: Connection▼
Belonging without blending. Enter the sanctuary where urban meets nature.
4. How confident do you feel in managing work-related demands and communication without compromising wellbeing?
Stage 5: Breath▼
Travel toward the coast. Wide skies and sea air. Freedom to Be.
5. If you had access to holistic coaching and energy tools, what challenges would you hope it would help you address most?
