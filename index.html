Oubaitori Trail

桜梅桃李 (Oubaitori): Bloom in your own time.
Stage 1: Reset▼
Wellness System Check-inHoughton-le-Spring: Focus on breath and stillness. The trail doesn't need to rush before growth.
Stage 2: Expansion▼
Create SpaceWashington or Durham: Move toward expansive views. Reflect on your capacity to grow.
Stage 3: Flow▼
Fluid PathsAngel of the North or Seaham: Allow movement and reflection. 'Be' in the current.
Stage 4: Connection▼
Belonging without BlendingJesmond Dene or Castle Eden Dene: Where urban meets nature. Find stillness in the heart of the region.
Stage 5: Breath▼
Freedom to BeNorthumberland or Saltburn: Travel toward the coast. Wide skies and sea air.
