Oubaitori Blossom Trail

The Japanese concept of Oubaitori (桜梅桃李) reminds us that the Cherry, Plum, Peach, and Apricot all bloom in their own time and way. No flower competes with another; each honors its own unique rhythm [11, 14].
1. Non-Comparison The plum does not compete with the cherry to bloom first [8].
2. Self-Acceptance Embrace your inherent "colors" and traits [8].
3. Trust the Process Growth happens in its own God-ordained or natural season [8, 14].
