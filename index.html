Oubaitori Blossom Trail

The Journey as a Living Programme
Based on the Japanese principle 桜梅桃李 (Oubaitori): Just as the Cherry, Plum, Peach, and Apricot bloom in their own time, each journey is unique.
The 7 Stages of the Trail
Stage 1: Reset

Begin with a wellness system check-in. Breath, body, stillness. No destination urgency.

Stage 2: Expansion

Move toward Penshaw Monument. Blossom here is wide open, expansive. Space to integrate everything.

Stage 3: Flow

Head into Durham along the River Wear. Blossom reflects water—an emotional, fluid path.

Stage 4: Connection

Enter Jesmond Dene. A blend of urban life and nature. Blossom balances our environment.

Stage 5: Breath

Travel north toward the Northumberland Coast. Wide skies, sea air, and wild coastal blossom.

Stage 6: Signature Message

Oubaitori narrative to be added.

Stage 7: Closing Vision

Houghton-le-Spring: Growth doesn't require pressure. A new landmark trail where space to blossom truly exists.
