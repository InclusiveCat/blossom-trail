OUR VISION ▼
Supporting neurodivergent adults to recover, regulate and thrive. Bloom at your own pace, supported by a collective where no one walks alone.
YOUR JOURNEY BEGINS ▼
A soft, grounding welcome.
Technique: 5-4-3-2-1
Identify 5 items you see, 4 things you can touch, 3 sounds you hear, 2 scents you can smell, and 1 flavour you can taste. Anchors you in the present.
A MOMENT TO CHECK IN ▼
Invites awareness without pressure.
Technique: Box Breathing
Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds. Regulates heart rate.
UNDERSTANDING WHAT YOU NEED ▼
Noticing what would bring you the most comfort and ease right now.
Technique: Wrist Cooling
Run cold water over wrists for 30s to help reset a triggered nervous system.
UNDERSTANDING YOUR CAPACITY ▼
Respecting your natural rhythm and where your energy sits today.
Technique: Firm Pressure
Give yourself a firm hug. Deep pressure helps the brain process where the body is in space.
RECOGNISING YOUR STRENGTHS ▼
A shift toward resilience, agency, and thriving.
Technique: Humming
Hum a low note to feel the vibration in your chest. Stimulates the vagus nerve to soothe stress.
EXPLORING WHAT COULD SUPPORT YOU ▼
Opening up to the people, spaces, and ideas that can walk alongside you.
Technique: Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR)
By tensing and relaxing different muscle groups, you can discharge stored tension and increase body awareness.
FINDING WHAT IS RIGHT FOR YOU ▼
In your own time, at your own pace, no comparison.
Technique: Shaking It Off (Neurogenic Tremors)
Deliberately shaking your arms, legs, or whole body for 1 to 2 minutes can help release excess adrenaline and restore a sense of calm.
